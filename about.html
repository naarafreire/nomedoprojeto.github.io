Naara Freire

Hello, I'm a junior Multimedia Designer born in Brazil and currently based in Portugal. I graduated from ESAD.CR in 2024 and I find immense joy in pushing the boundaries of imagination and bringing my ideas to life through design. Being born in Brazil has infused a rich cultural perspective into my work, and I carry that diversity with pride. It's not just about creating visually appealing designs for me; it's about crafting experiences that resonate with people on a deeper level.
