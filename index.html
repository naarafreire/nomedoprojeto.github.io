About me

Hello, I'm a junior Multimedia Designer born in Brazil and currently based in Portugal. I graduated from ESAD.CR in 2024 and I find immense joy in pushing the boundaries of imagination and bringing my ideas to life through design. Being born in Brazil has infused a rich cultural perspective into my work, and I carry that diversity with pride. It's not just about creating visually appealing designs for me; it's about crafting experiences that resonate with people on a deeper level.

Projects

Trash Island
Trash Island is an immersive VR game designed to teach kids the importance of recycling through interactive adventures on a virtual island polluted with waste.

DateRate
DateRate is a user-friendly app that helps people discover new spots to go and allows them to rate and share their experiences.

Fox's House
One small isometric kitchen and the Charming Mr. Fox, modeled in Blender, designed for children's animation.

Cardboard Box
This 3D project of a cardboard box, was created to practice and analyze texture behavior for a personal project.

Too Many Tools, Too Little Time
"Too Many Tools, Too Little Time" is an AR project designed to provoke reflection on how relentless media consumption diminishes our intellectual engagement.

Tutto
The redesign of the game Tutto for a personal project aims to infuse it with a fresh, playful aesthetic to appeal more effectively to children.

I Love You So
The Spotify Canvas Animation for "I Love You So," crafted as part of a university project, integrates visuals to amplify the song's essence

Happiness
The Happiness Festival AR poster features a serotonin molecule as its central graphic element, modeled and animated in Blender to elevate user engagement and evoke a sense of connection.
